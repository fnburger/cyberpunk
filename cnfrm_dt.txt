Right-click with Healing Orb to heal yourself if you are damaged. Don’t be afraid to use this if you’re the player most in need of healing on your team. Healing Orb is one of the main reasons why Sage has the highest win rate for 1v1 fights of any Valorant agent.
There are a great many different uses for Barrier Orb during a round, but you only get one charge, so you must carefully choose how and when in the round to use it.
